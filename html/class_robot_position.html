static

Calculate the XYZ position of the robot given the winch geometries.

Knowing the origin point of each winch and the length of each line, calculate the current position of the robot. This assumes that O is located at (0,0,0), P is at (d,0,0) and Q is at (i,j,0). In other words, the origins are planar and P is on the x-axis.
